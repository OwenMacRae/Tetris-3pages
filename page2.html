What is Tetris?

Tetris is a rather simple game, all you do is move the falling blocks and reposition them with the goal of filling the horizontal lines. The blocks come in a variety of shapes and colours. You lose the game by letting the blocks reach the top of the screen, and interestingly enough it recently had a resurgence, with a game called Tetris 99 coming out on the Nintendo Switch, in which you play Tetris against other people.
