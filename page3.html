Anecdotes About Tetris!

In 2010 the first Classic Tetris World Championship was held in Los Angeles, California, where it was also held in 2011, but every year from 2012 and on it has been held in Portland, Oregon. It is a competitive tournament to see who is the best Tetris player in the world. The current world champion is a guy named Joseph Saelee who is a 18 year old American resident. He first won the title from a man named Jones Neubauer in 2018, and then defended his title in 2019.
