Tetris's History!

Tetris originated in Russia, Originally created by a man named Alexey Pajitnov in 1984. The game then got published by a plethora of companies, the most prominent of those being Nintendo. The game saw massive success, still being the number 1 selling game worldwide, selling over 495 million copies. Then in early 2019, Tetris surged in popularity when Tetris 99 came out, a version of Tetris where you can play against your friends and random people around the world in a battle royal style, this game is still actively played by around 3 million users, since is was released as a free game.
